We believe in solutions that can concurrently improve communities
and our schools to benefit the children that need it most.

EdBuild was created to rethink schools and rebuild communities.

Our team focuses on the needs of children in their schools and the broader community, and provide tangible solutions that support the revitalization of school districts, cities and states.

Together we can rebuild the future.

When neighborhoods suffer,
schools fail.

And when schools fail,
neighborhoods suffer.

To address this cycle, our work focuses on three key areas where economic development and education reform intersect.

Infrastructure

We believe that rightsizing building portfolios and providing high quality charter schools with unique financing opportunities, we can bring more resources to school districts and communities while increasing quality school options for families.

Workforce

We believe that smart compensation and pension systems will attract and retain the best teachers to inspire students in struggling schools and contribute to the local economy by living in the neighborhoods in which they teach.

Resources

We believe that adequate funding, efficient spending and access to alternative financing can spur innovation and ensure every public school student has the resources they need to succeed.

Our three-tiered approach will help leaders at every level, from district superintendents to city and state leaders.


edBuild is committed to:

  • Drive New Thinking

    Through the compilation of data, the encouragement of discourse and debate, and the development of innovative new communications strategies.

  • Reveal Pathways for Change

    By tracking emerging research, conducting unique independent analysis and partnering with respected peers to support the pursuit of evidence in these areas.

  • Implement Innovation

    By building actionable data-based models, and guiding stakeholders through the implementation of new and innovative strategies – every step of the way.
